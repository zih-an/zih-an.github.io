A Piece of Thoughts

发表于2021-05-27|更新于2021-05-27|感悟
|阅读量:

Recently, I just understood many things…

1. Interests go first.

No matter who you am, you really have some bias, what needs to be explored is what they really are. Once you find them, everthing turns to be interesting. Maybe there are many great persons, but you will never give up or underestimate yourself because of them. They will become your “idol”, someone you admire, that’s a kind of belief(though it may “crash”). Anyway, what’s the point of that? Keep doing your favourite things and Learn to admire others. This can not only develop your own confidence, but also your attitude that keeping low-key but great.

2. About start a business.

All great business starts from interests and a creative idea, which always associates with someone’s techniques in a specific domain. Absolutely, the specific domain’s techniques and interests are the most important things. For examples, Jackson Wang’s label Team Wang and the Ryce ent., they all start from Jackson and music. Jackson has sophiscated skills and such powerful influence in music and idol industry all over the world. With his passion, it is absolutely OK to start from himself and then other people. That’s how a great business starts. However, in this process, some helps from management and finance domain are necessary, but they aren’t from the very begining.

Now, let’s talk about another topic related to this…

3. About majors: Science and Engineering OR Business OR Arts

Introduction: I can’t fully understand 3 years ago, why it is widely said that, if you don’t have much “mine” in your family, don’t choose business. But I did change my major at the last moment all because of this. Though kind of regret after doing so, fortunately I’m really glad to do so. Yes, this is right.

As above, all starts from techniques in a specific domain, all starts from interests. The Business sides are only related to the “middle” process, but not the very begining and the final achievement. Apparently, majoring in science and engineering is the most easist to gain this. They have a really specific domain, they can build some creative things, and they are kind of unique techniques!!! Not just talking, not just writing rely on others, they are some spcific things like painting, singing, dancing… Once you get the techs, you can start your own business then. Something great starts, and become wider and wider.

4. About structures

emm… I’m really obsessed with perfect structures all the time. That leads to a really big problem, which is, I won’t take actions immediately if I don’t find the structures perfect enough. But apparently, everthing starts with many pieces and then a huge entity. The absolutely perfect structure doesn’t exist at the begining. So, just try to do with pieces…that’ll be great.

* A little conclusion…

The only key to success is interest.

Don’t have to feel inferior when seeing someone who is really great. Try to admire them, and keep doing what you really like with happiness. And try not to be very perfect at the begining.
